About us

We make advanced technology accessible to everyone

I'm Marcos, a developer and creator of digital solutions with over 5 years of experience helping people and businesses get the most out of their technology. My journey began when I realized that many technical solutions were locked behind expensive paywalls or required advanced knowledge that most people don't have.

That's why I created Kara Tech — to democratize access to high-quality technical solutions. Every tool we develop is free, open-source, and designed to be used by anyone, regardless of their technical background.

Our mission is simple: make your computer and internet work better, at no cost. We believe that optimizing technology should be a right, not a luxury.

Open innovation

All our code is open source. Anyone can inspect, modify, and improve it.

Full transparency

No hidden fees, no data collection, no surprises. Just honest solutions.

Real commitment

We're not just here to sell software. We're here to make a difference.
