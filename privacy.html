Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Compliant with RGPD / GDPR (EU) 2016/679

1. Who is responsible for your data?

Kara Tech — operated by Marcos (sole operator), based in Spain.

Contact for data protection matters: hello@karatech.dev

2. What data do we collect?

We only collect the minimum necessary data:

  • Contact form data: name, email address, and message content, when you voluntarily submit a contact request.
  • Cookie consent preferences: stored locally in your browser's localStorage (not on our servers).
  • Analytics data (if consented): anonymous usage statistics via Google Analytics (page views, session duration, country). No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Marketing data (if consented): anonymized advertising interaction data via third-party platforms such as Facebook Pixel.

🔒 We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Legal basis for processing

  • Legitimate interest: necessary cookies required for the site to function.
  • Consent: analytics and marketing cookies, only after you explicitly opt in.
  • Contractual necessity: contact form data, to respond to your enquiry.

4. How long do we keep your data?

  • Contact form submissions: deleted within 12 months of last contact.
  • Cookie consent record: stored in your browser for up to 1 year, then re-requested.
  • Analytics data: retained by Google Analytics for up to 26 months (configurable).

5. Who do we share your data with?

Only with trusted third-party processors, and only where you have given consent:

  • Google LLC — Analytics (if consented). Google Privacy Policy.
  • Meta Platforms Ireland Ltd — Facebook Pixel (if consented). Meta Privacy Policy.

We do not transfer your personal data outside the European Economic Area without appropriate safeguards.

6. Your rights under GDPR

As a data subject in the EU, you have the following rights:

AccessRequest a copy of the data we hold about you.
RectificationAsk us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
ErasureRequest deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
ObjectionObject to processing based on legitimate interest.
PortabilityReceive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Withdraw consentWithdraw cookie consent at any time via our cookie settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@karatech.dev. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with Spain's data protection authority, the AEPD (Agencia Española de Protección de Datos).

7. Cookies

For detailed information about the cookies we use, please visit our Cookie Policy page.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For any questions or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@karatech.dev.
