Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At Kara Tech, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide to us through our contact form: your name, email address, and the message you send. We do not collect any personal information automatically through cookies or tracking technologies.

How we use your information

The information you provide is used solely to respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Email communications

When you contact us via email or the contact form, we will use your email address to respond to your message. We do not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in.

Third-party services

Our contact form uses Formspree to process and forward messages to our email. Formspree's privacy policy applies to the submission process. We recommend you review their policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Data security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal information that we hold. To exercise these rights, please contact us at karateckwinopt@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact us
